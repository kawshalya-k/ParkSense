About ParkSense

Our Mission

ParkSense aims to eliminate the frustration of finding parking spots by leveraging cutting-edge AI and computer vision technology. We make parking simple, fast, and stress-free.

How It Works

1

CCTV Monitoring

Our AI analyzes camera feeds to detect available spots in real-time

2

View & Reserve

Browse available spots on our interactive map and reserve instantly

3

Park with Ease

Your spot is held for 15 minutes - plenty of time to arrive and park

The Technology

ParkSense uses machine learning models trained on thousands of parking lot images to accurately detect vehicle presence. Combined with Django's robust backend and real-time updates, we provide a seamless parking experience.
